World of Rugby

Welcome to the World of Rugby Memory Game. Please select one of the options below.

The object of the game is to find all the matching pairs in the least amount of moves before the time runs out. A player makes a match if the two cards flipped are identical. If the cards do not match they will flip back. There are three difficulty levels for a player to challenge themselves:

  • Amateur - 16 cards
  • Professional - 20 cards
  • Legend - 24 cards
